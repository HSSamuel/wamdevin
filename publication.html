Publications & Resources

Disseminating knowledge for capacity building and development.

The WAMDEVIN Knowledge Hub

WAMDEVIN prepares a wide range of publications distilled from research commissioned by member institutions. Our goal is to establish institutional mechanisms for disseminating and exchanging relevant information within the sub-region.

Through our revived newsletters, journals, and handbooks, we stimulate dialogue between professionals, governments, and the private sector on critical management issues.
