Membership

Join the premier network of Management Development Institutes in West Africa.

Why Join WAMDEVIN?

Membership offers your institution unparalleled access to regional expertise, funding opportunities, and a platform to influence public policy across ECOWAS nations.

  • Global & Regional Recognition

    Gain visibility through our affiliation with the Commonwealth Secretariat and African Union bodies.

  • Capacity Building

    Access subsidized training for faculty and administrative staff (e.g., Train-the-Trainers).

  • Research Grants

    Participate in funded multi-country research projects on governance and development.

  • Networking

    Collaborate with 16+ leading MDIs and business schools for staff/student exchange.

Eligibility

Open to Management Development Institutes (MDIs), Public Administration Colleges, and accredited Business Schools in West Africa.
